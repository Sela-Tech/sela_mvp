Sela Tutorial

Sela takes your project to the next level! Simply register your project with a description and budget and Sela will take care of the rest.

  • 1. Create Project

    Provide description of the project, including the locale, the amount and type of human capital needed, the projected deadline, and the budget.

  • 2. Track Project

    Once a project has been created, the administrator can monitor the developments of the project. Some of the critical analytics of interest include the number of workers registered to complete the project, a rough progress indication, and daily statistics on the amount of work completed on a given workday.

  • 3. Review Project

    When the deadline for a project has been reached, the administrator can review the data and decide whether or not they are willing to continue funding the project. This step should ideally result in healthy business partnerships in the future.
