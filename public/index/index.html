About Sela

Lack of accountability and transparency undermines global economic and human development.

Sela is a payments, contracts and distributed communication platform built upon a blockchain backbone. Platform participants collect and distribute value and information across a secure, transparent and reputation-driven network. Participants include governments, global corporations, financial institutions, investors, healthcare networks, foundations, NGOs, community groups, civic organizations, journalists and individuals creating opportunity across the world.

Read more about the Sela vision here!

Powered by DLT

Distributed Ledger Technology (DLT) is increasingly becoming recognized as the eventual solution to many socioeconomic and institutional woes in today's society. As the foundation behind Bitcoin, DLT enables a certified society in which one can trust information without needing to trust individuals.

The merits of DLT have been substantiated through swift payment systems, like Dash; secure file transfer protocols, like Filecoin, and now micropayment services, like Sela. The potential for DLT is as vast as it is exciting and Sela is forging the path for a new generation of socially responsible solutions in this vibrant, new space.
